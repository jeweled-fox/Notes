Help

General use

Notepad is a simple online note-taking tool, with all features provided 100% free, instantly.

Saving tools

"Save" saves your notes to your browser's local storage.

"Load" loads your notes from your browser's local storage.

"Clear save" deletes all your notes and Advanced option preferences from your browser's local storage.

"Save to file" allows you to save your notes to your device as a text file.

Text tools

"Paste text from clipboard" pastes the text you have copied on your clipboard into your notes.

"Copy text to clipboard" copies your notes to your clipboard.

"Clear notes" clears your current notes. This does NOT affect your saved notes.

"Insert Bullet" allows you to insert various types of bullet points into your notes, such as normal bullet points and various types of arrows.

"Font" allows you to select the font that your notes are displayed in. Currently, there are five different fonts that you can choose from:

  • Default (your browser's default font for text areas)
  • Roboto
  • Handwriting (official name Handlee)
  • Fancy (official name Quattrocento)
  • Thin (official name Khand)

Advanced Options

These are options that you can set if you need specific functions to work. You don't need to use them if you don't want to.

"Autoload locally saved notes" makes it so your locally saved notes automatically load when you open the page.

"Automatically save notes on clear" makes it so that when you clear your notes, it automatically saves them to your browser's local storage.

"Autosave notes" makes it so that every minute, your notes automatically save (it's the equivalent of manually pressing the "Save" button). You can set the interval so it can save every minute (the default), every five minutes, every ten minutes, or every thirty minutes.

FAQ

"Why did I lose my locally saved notes?"

You may have cleared your browser cookies or local storage. The notepad uses those to store your notes.

"Why aren't the copy/paste tools working?"

You or your browser may have blocked the clipboard permission. Please set the permission to "allow" (You can find the permission in Google Chrome by clicking the lock to the left of the website address.).

"Am I safe if I put sensitive information here?"

Sensitive information is safe here. Your notes are only saved locally (to your browser), and the website won't store it on a server. HOWEVER, because of this, sensitive information can be lost. Do not save it soley here.

"It says that the last autosave is "null". What does this mean?"

That just appears if you haven't used the autosave feature before. Once you use it, a date will pop up.
