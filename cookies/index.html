Our Use of Cookies

On our site, we use cookies (in the form of local browser storage) when you save your notes.

Because of this, if you have cookies blocked on this site, it won't work correctly.

We don't use cookies to track you or target ads (because there are no ads here).

What we use cookies for:

  • Saving your notes, when you manually save them or turn autosave on.
  • Saving your preferences for Advanced options.

Other things to note:

We access your clipboard to copy or paste your notes when you choose to. If you have the permission(s) blocked, these features may not work.

Other cookies may be for the external social links, not created or managed by us.
